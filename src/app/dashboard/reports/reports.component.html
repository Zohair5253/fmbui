This is an MVP (Minimum Viable Product) for Faizul Mawaid al Burhaniyah management. Using which one can easily track the activities regarding Faizul Mawaid al Burhaniyah on day to day basis.

Faizul Mawaid al Burhaniyah is chosen as a use case with an intent to have a real-world example to display features which could also be helpful in other scenarios as well.

For example: Maintaining Employees salary / leaves and other data, Maintaining stock of various items in your shop etc.

Similarly, there can be N number of use cases in which such applications will work as a catalyst and ease your day to day tasks.
